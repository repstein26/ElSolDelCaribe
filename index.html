El Sol Del Caribe

   El Sol Del Caribe means the Sun of the Caribbean, here is a place to bring you the taste of Dominican Republic at amazing prices with nice portions. The restaurant began its legacy 17 years ago and quickly took its place in Jersey City’s vibrant community. The restaurant has won awards for serving and connecting with the community such as the police department, fire department, and other local businesses. The restaurant is well known among college students for loving El Sol Del Caribe's delicious food. You will be welcome by a buffet of ready variety hot foods with a selection of combo deals available. You don’t know Jersey City if you have never eaten at El Sol del Caribe.
